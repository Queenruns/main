    Run For Us

    If you're a BA enthousiast/Pet hunter and would like to make some coin doing your favorite minigame, we can help with that.

    Things we look for in a trial.

    Attacker

    90+ range / 90+ hitpoints / 75+ attack and defence / 90+ strength.

    Display knowledge of the efficient kill order waves 1-4.

    Effectively execute the A/C block.

    Force spawning yellow egg.

    Duping the omega egg for 1 egg queens.

    (Elite) void, Dark bow, Crystal halberd, Dinh's Bulwark...

    Expected to have Scrawled Note (MM2) / Shrink-me-quick in near future if not already for forcing wave ends and red egg duping.

    Collector

    (Usually not trialled, but expected to learn.)

    70+ defence / 75+ hitpoints.

    Stacking fighters in the A/C block.

    Spam collect for h/c rounds.

    Loading red eggs on a/d rounds.

    1 egging and loading from correct place.

    Tank gear / Negative defensive gear.

    Healer

    Effective use of heal codes.

    Blocking last runner on waves 2 & 3.

    Vialling on waves 8-9 when client is h/c.

    Defender

    (Mechanics are valued over time)

    1 tick multikilling.

    Trap, trail and main-stack placement.

    Luring the last two healers.

    Staggering 2-x-2.

    Recovering double westies.

    2 logging wave 9.

    Stalling on waves 5+.

    To pass your trial your time needs to be sub 16 minutes. If you feel comfortable in your role and know of the techniques above (they don't have to be perfect), you should hop in the cc and ask for a trial.
